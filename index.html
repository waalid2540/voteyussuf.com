About Yussuf

Meet the leader our community needs

A Proven Leader for Our Community

Yussuf Abdi brings over 15 years of dedicated community service and leadership experience to the City Council race. As a longtime resident and active community member, Yussuf understands the challenges facing our neighborhoods and has the proven track record to deliver real solutions.

Experience & Qualifications:

  • Community Organizer and Advocate
  • Small Business Owner
  • Non-Profit Board Member
  • Youth Mentorship Program Leader
  • Neighborhood Watch Coordinator

Personal Background:

Born and raised in our community, Yussuf is a proud family man, entrepreneur, and community advocate. He holds a degree in Public Administration and has dedicated his career to public service and community development.

Our Platform

Real solutions for the issues that matter most

Affordable Housing

Expanding affordable housing options and supporting first-time homebuyers to ensure everyone can find a place to call home in our community.

Economic Development

Attracting new businesses, supporting local entrepreneurs, and creating good-paying jobs to strengthen our local economy.

Public Safety

Working with law enforcement and community organizations to build safer neighborhoods through prevention and community engagement.

Infrastructure

Improving roads, sidewalks, and public transportation to connect our community and support sustainable growth.

Education

Investing in our schools, after-school programs, and educational opportunities to give every child the chance to succeed.

Environment

Promoting clean energy, green spaces, and sustainable practices to protect our environment for future generations.
